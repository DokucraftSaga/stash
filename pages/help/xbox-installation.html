Installing Dokucraft on Xbox

Due to various problems with putting Dokucraft on the in-game store, installing the pack on Xbox is a bit trickier than on PC, but it is still possible by following the instructions below.

Note: This process requires a USB drive.

On PC

  1. Download the Bedrock edition of the resource pack: Light – High – Dark

  2. Open it using 7zip or WinRAR

  3. Create a new folder somewhere on your PC called "Dokucraft" and place all of the files from the pack in that folder

  4. Put the Dokucraft folder on a USB drive

On Xbox

  1. Go to Xbox Settings > Display & Sound > Video Output > Video Fidelity & Overscan, then untick the checkbox for "Apps can add a Border"

  2. Plug your USB drive into your Xbox

  3. Open File Explorer

  4. Click the USB icon on the left side of the screen

  5. Copy the Dokucraft folder

  6. Go to the Xbox Store and search for UFO.Transfer, and install it

  7. Open the app, then click on the gear in the top right of the screen

  8. Click "Open in File Explorer", then click Packages at the top of the screen

  9. Find "Microsoft.MinecraftUWPConsole" and open it, click LocalState > games > com.mojang

  10. Make a new folder named "resource_packs" in all lowercase

  11. Press Start on the folder and click Paste

  12. Open Minecraft and click Play, edit the world you want to use it on and apply it
